Kenyan Chapatis; Golden, soft and layered

Chapati is a soft, layered flatbread popular in Kenyan homes.
It’s a staple often served with stews, vegetables, or even enjoyed with tea.
Its golden, flaky texture makes it a true comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Extra flour for rolling
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide into small balls and roll each into a circle.
  5. Cook on a hot pan with a light brush of oil until golden on both sides.

Serve warm with your favorite stew or vegetables and enjoy the classic Kenyan meal.
