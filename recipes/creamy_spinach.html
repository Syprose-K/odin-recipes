Creamy Spinach Recipe- Hotel Standard

Ever wondered how hotels make delicious creamy spinach?
Now you can make it at home!
Creamy spinach is a rich, flavorful dish that turns simple greens into a delicious side.
With onions, garlic, and a touch of cream, it’s a perfect blend of healthy and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh spinach (washed and chopped)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • 1/2 cup cream (or heavy milk)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil/butter in a pan, sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. Stir in cream and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer 3–5 minutes until creamy, then serve.

Pair creamy spinach with chapati, rice, or roasted meats for a wholesome meal.
