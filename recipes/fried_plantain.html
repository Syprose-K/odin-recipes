Fried Plantains Recipe

Fried plantains are a sweet and savory East African favorite.
They’re golden, caramelized on the outside, and soft on the inside.
Perfect as a snack or a side dish with meals.

Ingredients

  • 2–3 ripe plantains, yellow with black spots are the best
  • Cooking oil for frying
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Peel the plantains and slice them diagonally into medium-thick pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Fry plantain slices until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Remove and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  5. Sprinkle with a little salt if desired.

Serve hot as a snack, breakfast side, or alongside beans, rice, or stew.
