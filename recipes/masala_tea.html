Aromatic Masala Tea

Masala tea is a spiced Kenyan favorite that blends tea with cardamom, ginger, cinnamon, and cloves.
It’s warming, aromatic, and best enjoyed hot.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons tea leaves (or 2 tea bags)
  • 3–4 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2–3 cloves
  • 1 small piece fresh ginger (crushed)
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water with spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger).
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Pour in milk and bring to a gentle boil.
  4. Add sugar to taste.
  5. Strain into cups and serve hot.

Pour into cups and sip slowly taking in the flavors.
A comforting drink for any time of day.
