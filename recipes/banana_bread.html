Sweet Banana Bread Recipe

This banana bread is soft, moist, and has a sweet banana flavor.
It is the perfect way to make use of overripe bananas sitting

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon basking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°C (350Â°F).
  2. Mash bananas in a large bowl and mix with melted butter.
  3. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in baking soda, salt, and flour until combined.
  5. Pour batter into a greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 1 hour. Cool before serving.

And done! I hope your banana bread turned out as delicious as mine.
