🍌 Banana Bread

Intro: This banana bread is soft, moist, and packed with sweet banana flavor. It’s the perfect way to use up overripe bananas sitting in your kitchen. Enjoy it fresh from the oven as a comforting snack or breakfast treat.

Prep: 10–15 min Cook: 50–60 min Serves: 8–10

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1/3 cup melted butter or oil
  • 1/2–3/4 cup sugar (to taste)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • (Optional) 1/2 cup chocolate chips or chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a loaf tin.
  2. Whisk mashed bananas, melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in baking soda, salt, then flour until just combined. Fold in add-ins.
  4. Pour into tin and bake 50–60 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean.
  5. Cool 10–15 minutes, then slice and serve.

Tips

  • Use very ripe (spotty) bananas for best flavor.
  • Don’t overmix once you add flour—this keeps it tender.
  • Wrap leftovers; it’s even better the next day.
