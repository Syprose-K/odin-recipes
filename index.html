Kiche Recipes

  1. Banana Bread🍌🍞

    2. This banana bread is soft, moist, and has a sweet banana flavor.
    It is the perfect way to make use of overripe bananas sitting in your kitchen.
    Enjoy it fresh from the oven as a comforting snack or breakfast treat.🍞🍞🍌

  2. Fried Plantains

    3. Fried plantains that are crispy on the outside and soft, sweet on the inside.
    Perfect snack or side for breakfast or as a sweet treat.🍌🍌🍌

  3. Creamy Spinach

    4. A rich and comforting side dish packed with flavors and nutrients.
    Simmered in smooth velvet sauce made with creams and seasonings.
    Can be served with meat, rice, bread, or ugali.

  4. Kenyan Chapati🍪

    5. A Kenyan favorite, soft, layered flatbread rich in flavor and flaky in texture.
    Perfect for breakfast, dinner or alongside curries or stews.🍪🍪

  5. Masala Tea

    6. Popular in Kenya and South Asia.
    Masala tea is black tea spiced with aromatic spices then simmered with milk and sweetener.☕️☕️
    It is very rich in flavors and the rich aroma is unforgetable.

  6. Mandazi

    7. An East African delicacy, golden on the outside and fluffy on the inside.
    Perfect with tea or coffee.
